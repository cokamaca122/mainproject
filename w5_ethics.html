Ethics in Research

Introduction

While preparing the ethics application for the research ethics board at my study site, I realized how complex and detailed this process was. This made me interested not only in exploring Canada's general ethical research guidelines but also in looking at how I have previously handled ethical challenges. In this section, I will share three practical examples from my own experiences.

Conclusion
