Data Analysis

Introduction

I have been curious about how data analysis works in qualitative research, especially since I have only used standardized questionnaires, where the analysis follows an established statistical procedure. However, the data that is in the form of words and stories may be more complex. Therefore, in this section, I am looking into the principles of qualitative data analysis, providing a detailed video presentation.

Additionally, I have included a fun, interactive game based on narrative analysis to explore how stories are examined and understood in research settings.

After reading Principles, and Game subsections, please come back and click here to read the conclusion.

Conclusion

After exploring qualitative data analysis, I could see that although it may look complicated, it still follows a systematic process, from coding to interpreting themes. I also learned an important aspect: it relies on a careful, iterative approach rather than a generalized formula. Narrative analysis is a good example of how these principles actually work in practice, focusing on both the structure and the meaning of people's stories. While creating the game on narrative analysis, I learned that the way people tell their stories is as important as the context of those stories. This method seems especially relevant to disability and rehabilitation, where the manner in which the stories are presented is valuable to data analysis. Even though I am new to qualitative analysis, I can now understand how to work with words and stories systematically, as well as the advantages of such an approach. Also, I hope that in the future, I will be able to apply these steps in a study with my own participants should I decide to implement a qualitative approach.
